    Jacob presented at the RLC 2024 CoCoMARL Workshop!

    Jacob gave a poster presentation of our paper “Coordination Machines for Minimizing Communication in Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning” at the Coordination and Cooperation in Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning (CoCoMARL) Workshop at the Reinforcement Learning Conference (RLC) 2024! We present a hierarchical method for minimizing communications subject to mission success requirements.
