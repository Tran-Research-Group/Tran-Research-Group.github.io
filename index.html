    Tran Research Group

    Advancing autonomy through interdisciplinary research.

    Welcome! Our goal is to advance research on autonomous and intelligent systems with a focus on robots that interact with each other and humans. We primarily work at the intersection of robotics, artificial intelligence, and multi-agent systems, though we draw inspiration from other fields, like neuroscience, as well.
